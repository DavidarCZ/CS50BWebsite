Talk to Us

Share Your Experience

Your story matters. Sharing your experience with anxiety can help others feel less alone and provide hope to those who are struggling.

Anonymous Support

You can reach out to us anonymously if you need someone to listen. We're here to provide support and understanding.

Anonymous Email: listen@understandinganxiety.org

Peer Support Network

Connect with others who understand what you're going through. Our peer support network provides a safe space to share and receive encouragement.

Feedback and Suggestions

Help us improve our resources. If you have suggestions for topics to cover or ways we can better support those with anxiety, we'd love to hear from you.

Feedback Email: feedback@understandinganxiety.org

Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful and kind to others
  • Share experiences, not medical advice
  • Respect privacy and confidentiality
  • No spam or promotional content

Remember

While peer support is valuable, it's not a replacement for professional help. If you're in crisis or need immediate assistance, please reach out to a mental health professional or crisis hotline.
