Getting Help for Anxiety

When to Seek Professional Help

Consider reaching out to a mental health professional if:

  • Anxiety interferes with daily activities
  • You avoid situations due to anxiety
  • Physical symptoms are frequent or severe
  • You feel overwhelmed most days

Types of Professional Help

Therapists: Can provide counseling and coping strategies

Psychiatrists: Medical doctors who can prescribe medication if needed

Support Groups: Connect with others who understand your experience

Crisis Resources

If you're in crisis, help is available 24/7:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: 911

Online Resources

Many websites offer self-help tools and information about anxiety disorders.
