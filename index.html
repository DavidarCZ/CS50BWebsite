You're Not Alone

If you're struggling with anxiety, know that you're not alone. Millions of people experience anxiety, and it's completely normal. With the right tools and support, anxiety can be managed effectively. You have the strength to overcome this, and help is always available.

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease about something with an uncertain outcome. It's normal to feel anxious sometimes, but when anxiety becomes overwhelming, it can affect daily life.

Common Signs of Anxiety

Physical Symptoms

  • Racing or pounding heart
  • Sweating or trembling
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Muscle tension
  • Stomach problems

Emotional Symptoms

  • Excessive worry or fear
  • Feeling restless or on edge
  • Irritability
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Sleep problems

Types of Anxiety

General Anxiety

Persistent worry about everyday situations and events, often without a specific cause.

Social Anxiety

Fear of social situations and being judged by others. This can make it difficult to interact with people or speak in public.

Performance Anxiety

Nervousness about performing tasks or activities in front of others, such as giving presentations or taking tests.
